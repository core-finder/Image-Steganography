Image Steganography

Within a matter of seconds, you have the ability to conceal and retrieve messages within an image. Follow these simple steps: upload a file, enter the message you wish to hide, and click the "Hide" button. If you want to retrieve messages from these images, upload the image containing the hidden text, and click the "Read" button. This user-friendly process ensures quick and effortless message hiding and retrieval.
